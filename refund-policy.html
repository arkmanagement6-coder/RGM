Refund Policy

At Revanta Growth Media, we strive to provide high-quality digital services. Because our services are digital and involve significant labor and non-tangible assets, we have a clear refund policy.

Digital Marketing & Ads

Ad spend paid directly to platforms like Google or Meta is non-refundable. Management fees may be partially refundable if work has not yet commenced on the campaign strategy.

Web Development

Once a project has moved into the design and development phase, payments made are non-refundable. If a project is cancelled before work begins, a partial refund may be issued minus processing fees.

Service Satisfaction

We work closely with our clients to ensure satisfaction through multiple revision cycles as per the agreed project scope.
